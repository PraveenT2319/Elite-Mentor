About - Elite Mentor

“Elite Mentors” provides end to end solutions for those aspiring for study abroad. In this endeavour, students have to perform well in studies, take standardized tests and improve profile. Elite Mentors is best equipped in providing all these to a student along with Admission Counselling and VISA counselling.

Services offered

Test Prep : SAT, ACT, AP, AMC, GRE, GMAT, TOEFL and IELTS.

We also teach various subjects
(IGCSE, IB, As&A, MYP, ICSE) on Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, Business Studies, Accounts, Psychology, Languages and so on.

Under Admission counselling, we help students in shortlisting universities, writing Statement of Purpose, additional Essays, Letters of Recommendation, framing Bio Data, assist in providing internships, preparing student in facing VISA counselling,

Why Elite Mentors?

Our trainers have vast experience in teaching and are passionate about it, who understand the student’s exact requirements and thereby provide training which is apt to a student’s needs and capacity.

We have done a lot of study on the different types of questions and different ways of approaching to an answer, which will help the student to think in more than one way. We firmly believe that education must be enjoyable in order to learn fast.

We at “Elite Mentors” believe that our training will be the best in the industry. We assure you that our method will surely enhance the confidence of the individual.
