  • Even GMAT students are encouraged to sit for Vocabulary Classes
  • Students are taught to solve a problem in more than one approach (Short cut and traditional method)

About Our Academy

Each lesson a step, each step towards greatness.

“Elite Mentors” is a test prep centre started by passionate trainers in 2012. We cater to students preparing for study overseas such as SAT, ACT, SAT Subject tests,AP,GRE, GMAT, TOEFL and IELTS. We also provide coaching for students studying in International schools (IGCSE, IB, As&A) on Math, Science, Economics, Business studies, Accounts etc.

Why Elite Mentors?

We have a vast experience in the field of training and we are passionate about it. We exactly know the student’s requirements and a student’s understanding capacity and thereby provide training which is apt to a student’s needs and capacity.

We have done a lot of study on the different types of questions and different ways of approaching to an answer, which will help the student to think in more than one way. We firmly believe that education must be enjoyable in order to learn fast. Our way of teaching will be enjoyable as well as educational.

We at “Elite Mentors” believe that our training will be one of the best, if not ‘the best’ in the industry. We assure you that our training will surely enhance the confidence of the individual.

Once you enter “Elite Mentors”, it is a one way street. There is no exit. You will be a part of us for ever. So training is one part, the student can approach us for any doubts or clarifications at our premises at any time thereafter. Our success lies in our trainee’s success and our happiness lies in our trainee’s happiness.

Elite Mentor’s success rate is very high. Our average improvement in Old SAT was 375 points. The average improvement in the new SAT is close to 250. Our highest score in Old SAT was 2400/2400, in the new SAT, it is 1560/1600 and in ACT it is 35/36. In GRE, the highest score is 334/340 and in GMAT it is 770/800. A few of our students have studied in Ivy Leagues and many have availed scholarships and tuition fee waivers.
